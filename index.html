What is bullyism

Bullying is an unwanted behavior among school aged children.
Bullyism is a term used to describe the behavior of a bully which involves using strength or power to intimidate or harm people and it is also a practice

What is individualistic facts on bullyism

Being targeted by a bully can have significant consequence Bullying is a pervasive, complex problem that many kids encounter at school or in their daily lives.
If bullying is left unaddressed, other issues can crop up, including low self-esteem, academic problems, depression, and other mental health conditions.

Types of bullyism

Verbal Bullying

Verbal bullying:is when someone uses words to hurt or scare another person..Bullying can effect everyone those who are bullied,those who bully and those who witness bullying,bullying is linked to many negative out come including impacts on mental health,substance use and suicide.
  • Threats
  • Teasing
  • Name-calling
  • Discriminatory comments

    • How to stop verbal bullying

    1. Seek support from trusted individuals
    2. Defend by staying calm
    3. Dismiss the person's attack
    4. Play the kind parent

    Physical Bullying

    Physical is a type of bullying where someone uses their body or any object to hurt or scare someone else/to intimidate and gain control over a target.Physical bullying can run the gamut of everything from milder forms of harassment, such as pushing, shoving, or encroaching upon a student’s personal space, to all-out assaults that could land a child in the hospital. Physical bullying is more common among boys, but girls experience it too.

    Examples of Physical bullying:

    • Damaging someones property
    • Hitting
    • Shoving
    • Kicking
    • Pinching

    How to stop Physical Bullying

    1. Address the behavior
    2. Avoid participation in any form of bullying
    3. Express kindness to the bully's target
    4. Enforce consequences
    5. Reach out to school staff,parents,or an adult
